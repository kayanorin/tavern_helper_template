    [Campus Gates]
    - Gate of Glory (South Main Gate): The most magnificent ceremonial main entrance, used for major assemblies and float parades, leading straight to Iron Lily Plaza.

    - Valkyrie Parking Deck: An outdoor and multi-level parking complex with exceptionally wide spaces, perfectly suited for the large cars driven by female students.
    - The Conservatory Drop-off & Underground Garage: A luxurious valet-level facility featuring tinted UV-glass, full-length mirrors, and flattering fill lights.
